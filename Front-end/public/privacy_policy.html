PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 5 January 2026

Jurisdiction: Republic of South Africa

Compliance: Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

1. INTRODUCTION

Dankie (“we”, “us”, or “our”), operated by the Twelve Apostles Church in Trinity (TACT), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information when you use the Dankie mobile application and services.

By using Dankie, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information required to facilitate church administration, marketplace transactions, and academic assistance.

2.1 Personal Information

This includes your name, surname, contact number, email address, physical address, and church membership details (Branch/District).

2.2 Special Personal Information (Biometrics)

For Overseers and Committee Members, we collect biometric information (facial geometry and images) to perform mandatory identity verification and secure high-privilege accounts.

2.3 Financial Information

If you act as a Seller or Overseer, we collect banking details (Account Number, Bank Name, Branch Code) to facilitate payouts and track subscription payments. We do not store full credit card numbers; these are processed by secure third-party payment gateways.

2.4 TACTSO Academic Data

For students using TACTSO services, we collect academic records, matric results, and identification documents required for university applications.

2.5 Proxy Data (Third-Party Information)

Where you register an account on behalf of another person (e.g., an elderly member), we process that person’s data based on your warranty that you have obtained their informed consent.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We process your data for the following specific purposes:

  • Identity Verification: Using biometrics to prevent unauthorised access to church funds and administrative tools.
  • Church Administration: maintaining accurate membership rolls and district records.
  • Marketplace Transactions: Facilitating orders, deliveries, and payouts between Buyers and Sellers.
  • TACTSO Applications: Submitting university and funding applications on behalf of students upon their request.
  • Communication: Sending system alerts, church announcements, and status updates regarding orders or applications.

4. BIOMETRIC DATA PROCESSING POLICY

In strict compliance with POPIA regarding Special Personal Information:

  • Consent: Biometric data is only processed with explicit consent obtained during the setup of Overseer or Committee profiles.
  • Purpose Limitation: Biometric data is used solely for authentication and security. It is never sold, shared for marketing, or used for surveillance.
  • Retention: Biometric templates are deleted immediately upon the revocation of your Overseer or Committee status.

5. SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in operating the app (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processors), subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • With Educational Institutions: When you use TACTSO to apply for university, your data is shared with the specific institution you selected.
  • With Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of TACT and its members.

6. DATA SECURITY

We implement technical and organisational measures to secure your personal information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, strict access controls, and regular security audits. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and you use the Platform at your own risk.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention obligations (e.g., financial transaction records).
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information on reasonable grounds.

8. RETENTION OF DATA

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law (e.g., tax laws require retaining financial records for 5 years).

9. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the TACT Administration or use the "Help" feature within the app.
